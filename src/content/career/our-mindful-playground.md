### Our Mindful playground

Owner and Founder

Our Mindful Playground (OMP), is dedicated to bringing people together to achieve more and promoting wellness.

- Organising small events

- Large offsite events

- Health retreats in Portugal

- Podcasts

- Personal development plans

Events

Our team events are designed to provide a fun and exciting experience that encourages team bonding and reduces stress levels. We believe that by investing in your team's well-being, you can create a positive and productive work environment that benefits both employees and organisations.

Retreats

We also offer workshops and team events to promote team building, stress relief, and employee engagement. Our services range from on-site workshops to off-site experiences, including team events in Amsterdam and surf experiences further afield in Portugal.
