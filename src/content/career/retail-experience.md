### RETAIL EXPERIENCE - Multiple Brands

Cashier

As a dedicated and detail-oriented cashier in a fashion store, I excel at delivering outstanding customer service and ensuring smooth transactions.

My role involves managing sales, handling payments, and creating a welcoming and efficient checkout experience.

With a positive attitude and a keen eye for detail, I contribute to a pleasant shopping environment, driving customer satisfaction, loyalty and overall great customer experience.

Key Responsibilities

Customer Service

- Greet customers warmly as they approach the checkout counter, ensuring a positive and welcoming atmosphere.

- Assist customers with their purchases, addressing any questions about products or store policies.

- Handle customer inquiries and resolve complaints or refer them to the appropriate department for further assistance.

Transaction Processing

- Accurately process sales transactions, including cash, credit/debit card payments, and gift card redemptions.

- Scan items, apply discounts, and ensure correct pricing, providing receipts and packaging purchases.

Cash Handling

- Count and manage the cash drawer at the beginning and end of each shift, ensuring accuracy.

- Balance the cash drawer daily, investigating and resolving any discrepancies, and preparing cash for deposits.

Sales Support

- Promote ongoing sales, promotions, and loyalty programmes to enhance customer engagement.

- Maintain a clean and organised checkout area, fully stocked with necessary supplies.

Product Knowledge

- Stay informed about current store merchandise, promotions, and upcoming sales events.

- Advise customers on product features and benefits, aiding them in making informed purchasing decisions.

Compliance and Security

- Adhere to store policies and procedures to ensure compliance with regulations and laws.

- Verify customer identification for specific transactions as required, reporting any suspicious activities to management.

Team Collaboration

- Work collaboratively with store staff to ensure smooth store operations.

- Assist with restocking shelves, setting up displays, and other tasks as needed.

Customer Relationship Management

- Build and maintain positive relationships with customers, fostering a welcoming atmosphere that encourages repeat visits.

- Collect customer feedback and share insights with management to help improve store operations and customer satisfaction.

These responsibilities demonstrate my commitment to providing excellent customer service, facilitating accurate transactions, and contributing to a positive and efficient shopping experience.
