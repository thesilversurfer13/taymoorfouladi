### Snea-King

Founder

Startup online marketplace destination for sneaker products and events.

I managed everything from brand design, and website development to buying and selling, customer relations and logistics.

Key Responsibilities

Branding

- Develop Brand Identity: Create a unique brand image and voice that resonates with the target audience.

- Design Logo and Packaging: Ensure that the logo and packaging are appealing and reflective of the brand.

- Create Marketing Materials: Develop consistent branding materials including website design, business cards, and promotional items.

- Brand Positioning: Define the brand's position in the market to differentiate from competitors.

Social Media

- Social Media Strategy: Develop a comprehensive social media strategy to promote the brand.

- Content Creation: Generate engaging content including posts, stories, and videos for platforms like Instagram and Facebook.

- Community Engagement: Interact with followers, respond to comments, and build a community around the brand.

- Influencer Partnerships: Collaborate with influencers to increase brand visibility.

Finance

- Budget Management: Create and manage budgets for various business operations.

- Financial Planning: Forecast sales, expenses, and profit margins to ensure financial health.

- Bookkeeping: Maintain accurate financial records and oversee transactions.

- Funding and Investment: Seek and secure funding or investment opportunities.

Product Merchandising and Buying

- Market Research: Conduct research to understand market trends and customer preferences.

- Product Selection: Choose the right mix of products that appeal to the target market.

- Supplier Relations: Build and maintain relationships with suppliers for timely product procurement.

- Inventory Management: Manage stock levels to ensure product availability without overstocking.

Shipping

- Logistics Planning: Develop efficient shipping processes to ensure timely delivery of products.

- Carrier Selection: Choose reliable shipping carriers and negotiate shipping rates.

- Order Fulfilment: Oversee the order fulfilment process from order receipt to delivery.

- Returns Management: Establish and manage a return policy and process.

Physical Events

- Event Planning: Organise and plan physical events such as pop-up shops, product launches, and sneaker conventions.

- Venue Selection: Choose and secure appropriate venues for events.

- Marketing Events: Promote events through various channels to attract attendees.

- Event Execution: Manage event logistics, setup, and ensure smooth execution on the day of the event.
