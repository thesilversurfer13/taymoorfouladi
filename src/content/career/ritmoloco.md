### Ritmoloco

Ritmoloco

Established the bi-monthly Club night, Ritmoloco, gaining a respectable reputation amongst the student world in Manchester and hosting some world class DJ's, including Dj Rui Da Silva, with his class hit 'Touch Me'.

I Designed e a Flash website, Flyers, Posters & Stickers, whilst achieving Radio One & Student Direct recognition.

I was the main promoter, arranging flyer handouts, A0 posters being put up around Manchester, getting data for email lists and arranging staff.

Responsibilities

- Graphic design

- Web design

- Marketing

- DJ management

- Event management

- Budgeting
