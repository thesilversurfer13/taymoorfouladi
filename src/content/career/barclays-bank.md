## BARCLAYS

Bank Cashier

Barclays Bank Cashier

Dedicated and detail-oriented Bank Cashier with experience in managing daily financial transactions, providing exceptional customer service, and ensuring compliance with banking regulations.

Proficient in processing deposits, withdrawals, and payments, managing cash drawers, and assisting customers with their banking needs.

Known for strong numerical skills, accuracy, and the ability to handle customer inquiries and resolve issues efficiently.

Committed to maintaining the highest standards of service and contributing to the bank’s overall success.

Key Responsibilities

Customer Service

- Greet customers warmly and assist them with their banking needs.

- Answer customer inquiries regarding account balances, bank services, and policies.

- Resolve customer complaints or refer them to the appropriate department for further assistance.

Transaction Processing

- Process deposits, withdrawals, and payments accurately and efficiently.

- Handle cheque transactions, including deposits and cashing.

- Process loan payments and credit card payments.

- Issue cashier’s cheques, money orders, and bank drafts.

Cash Handling and Management

- Count and manage cash in the drawer at the beginning and end of each shift.

- Ensure the cash drawer balances at the end of the day, investigating and resolving any discrepancies.

- Prepare and package cash for transfer to the vault or other branches.

Account Maintenance

- Assist customers in opening and closing accounts.

- Update customer information and account details as needed.

- Provide information on available banking products and services to potential customers.

Compliance and Security

- Follow bank policies and procedures to ensure compliance with regulations and laws.

- Verify the identity of customers to prevent fraud and maintain security.

- Report any suspicious activity to the appropriate authorities.

Technical Proficiency

- Use banking software and systems to complete transactions and access customer information.

- Troubleshoot and resolve minor technical issues with banking equipment.

Sales and Referrals

- Promote bank products and services to customers based on their needs.

- Refer customers to other bank staff or departments for specialised services, such as loan officers or financial advisors.

Record Keeping and Reporting

- Maintain accurate records of transactions and customer interactions.

- Prepare daily, weekly, and monthly reports on cash transactions and customer activity.

Team Collaboration

- Work collaboratively with other bank staff to ensure smooth operation of the branch.

- Participate in training and development programs to stay updated on bank products, services, and policies.

General Administrative Duties

- Assist with general administrative tasks as needed, such as filing, answering phones, and managing office supplies.
