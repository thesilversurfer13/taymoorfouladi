Every morning, without fail, my dog greets me with a wagging tail and an eager bark, ready to start the day with boundless enthusiasm. This unwavering display of affection and loyalty always brightens my day, no matter how stressful it might be. As I pondered why my dog has such a profound impact on my mood, I realised that his behaviour offers valuable lessons in customer experience (CX). In this blog post, we'll explore how the principles my dog naturally embodies can be applied to create exceptional customer experiences that leave a lasting impression.
