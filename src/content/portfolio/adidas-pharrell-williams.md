## adidas x Pharrell Williams

Key Responsibilities

- Produce end to end digital campaign experience.

- Create, manage and deliver the campaigns, collaborating with multiple teams and departments across the business

- Oversee the design, UX, development, translations and QA

- Launch execution of the digital campaign.

- Utilising data and analytics to make informed decisions in real time to maximise click through and conversion rates.

- Lead a team of four Digital Experience managers and two Publishers in Germany and the Netherlands.

- Translating stakeholder input into digital marketing solutions, activated through our digital touch points

- Ensuring premium CX & UX across our digital touch points.

- Running retrospectives within global and markets teams to drive continued excellence.

- Workshop facilitation, face-to-face and virtually using online tools.

adidas x Pharrell Williams

Pharrell Williams is a groundbreaking artist, producer, songwriter, philanthropist, fashion designer, entrepreneur and founder of Humanrace™. The collaboration between adidas Originals and Pharrell Williams is an extension of Pharrell's vision to combine fashion, design and functionality for premium basics you can enjoy every day.
