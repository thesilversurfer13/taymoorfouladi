adidas x Y-3

Y-3 gives sporty styles a streetwear twist. Powered by adidas innovations and expressed in the style of Yohji Yamamoto, Y-3 creates progressive apparel, footwear and accessories—a vision the iconic sports brand shares with the famed designer. Founded in 2002, Y-3 marked the beginning of fashionable sportswear—and continues to focus on redefining the category. Nineteen years later, Y-3's signature style is all about combining innovative clothing with intensive performance features and mixing Yamamoto's unique elegance with timeless tailoring. The result is a modern streetwear uniform—designed in Japanese and German ateliers, made for the world.

Adidas' partnership with Y-3, a collaboration with Japanese designer Yohji Yamamoto, blends high fashion with sportswear. Launched in 2002, Y-3 stands for Yohji Yamamoto and the three stripes of Adidas, symbolising the fusion of innovative design and functional athletic wear. The partnership is known for its avant-garde aesthetics, combining Yamamoto's avant-garde style with Adidas' technical expertise. The collections feature minimalist designs, monochromatic colour palettes, and cutting-edge materials, creating a unique and fashionable sportswear line. This collaboration has been influential in the fashion industry, pushing the boundaries of traditional sportswear and establishing a new standard for stylish athletic apparel.
