## Value Retail — Digital Product Manager · Retail Digital Strategy & Customer Experience

**London · 2014 – 2018**

Directed digital platform strategy and omnichannel customer experience initiatives across a portfolio of global luxury retail destinations operated by Value Retail / The Bicester Village Shopping Collection.

### The challenge

Bring strategic, scalable digital product leadership to 9 international luxury villages — connecting the physical store estate, coach services, and digital touchpoints into a single seamless journey for millions of global visitors.

### What I did

- **Managed a £1M+ investment programme** to redesign and launch 9+ international retail websites — contributing to increased customer engagement, digital traffic, and omnichannel conversion opportunities.
- **Developed the strategic roadmap** to drive more physical traffic to the villages and increase customer acquisition.
- **Owned the end-to-end product lifecycle** — design, development, production, testing — and acted as the key decision-maker between Value Retail and external development agencies.
- **Managed a distributed team across 7 countries in Europe**, coordinating roadmap, backlog and BAU across markets.
- **Continually educated** the regional teams to fully utilise the digital products and rolled out new features and enhancements to the digital product backlog.
- Worked in **Agile environments** — sprint planning, user stories, backlog grooming, and KPI-driven iteration (user engagement, retention, revenue growth).

### Selected projects

- **Global website redesign** — new design, UX, UI and features across 9+ villages
- **Interactive village maps** and continuous BAU optimisation
- **Market-specific templates** to support content requirements across regions
- **User research programme** — interviews, surveys, usability testing to drive product decisions
- **Multilingual rollout** and translations across European markets

### Capabilities applied

`Product Management` · `Digital Strategy` · `UX / UI` · `Agile Delivery` · `Stakeholder Management` · `Multi-Market Rollout` · `CMS` · `Luxury Retail`
