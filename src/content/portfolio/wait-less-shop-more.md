## Wait Less, Shop More

Highights

CAMPAIGN HIGHLIGHTS

﻿﻿Campaign promoting the APP feature where consumers can book their slot for visiting the adidas stores and skipping the queue

﻿﻿Consumers can browse and book an 'arrival window on any enabled store.

ACTIVATION HIGHLIGHTS

com activation on HP, COVID-19 page displaying a scannable QR code - If a consumer already has the adidas APP clicking will take them directly to the booking page for the store,

﻿﻿If a consumer does not have the adidas APP installed, the app will take them to the app store and then immediately to the booking page

﻿﻿APP promotion

CX Challange

Continue to keep adidas stores open during the pandemic, whilst keeping both store staff and customers safe and adhearing to the Covid-19 guidlines.

How It Works

Consumers can book 7 days ahead of their visit

An arrival window of 15 minutes per hour

Key Responsibilities

- Produce end to end digital campaign experience.

- Create, manage and deliver the campaigns, collaborating with multiple teams and departments across the business

- Oversee the design, UX, development, translations and QA

- Launch execution of the digital campaign.

- Utilising data and analytics to make informed decisions in real time to maximise click through and conversion rates.

- Lead a team of four Digital Experience managers and two Publishers in Germany and the Netherlands.

- Translating stakeholder input into digital marketing solutions, activated through our digital touch points

- Ensuring premium CX & UX across our digital touch points.

- Running retrospectives within global and markets teams to drive continued excellence.

- Workshop facilitation, face-to-face and virtually using online tools.
